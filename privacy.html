Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

We Buy In FL ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use webuyinfl.com or contact us about your property, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you submit a form, call, or text us, we collect details such as your name, phone number, property address, and information about your property and situation.

How we use your information

  • To respond to your request and discuss a potential offer on your property.
  • To contact you by phone, text message, or call about your inquiry.

  • To meet legal, tax, and recordkeeping requirements.

Text message (SMS) consent

By providing your phone number, you agree that We Buy In FL may contact you by phone call and text message at that number regarding your property, including through automated means. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP, or reply HELP for help. We do not sell your phone number, and we do not share mobile information with third parties for their own marketing.

How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate (such as our form provider or title company) and only as needed to serve you, or when required by law.

Your choices

  • You may decline to provide information, though it may limit our ability to help.
  • You may opt out of text messages by replying STOP.
  • You may request that we delete the information you provided by contacting us at 941-405-3419.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information you share. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's privacy
